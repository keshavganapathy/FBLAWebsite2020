Frequent Fliers

Miles

When you join Omega Airlines, you can earn miles back on every flight. Join a family that will provide you the best comfort and amenities wherever your journey takes you. You get one mile per dollar spent, and your miles never expire! Choose the airline that is right for you. Choose Omega.

Check-In Easier

As a part of the multitude of benefits, we want to make your flight experience as enjoyable as possible. Enjoy your first check-in bag free 100% free, and 10% off all additional checked bags.

In-Flight Services

Even before your flight, you will begin to reap the benefits due to your commitment to Omega. We provide Main Cabin Priority Boarding as a part of the frequent flyers program! You will receive complimentary in-flight wifi, television, and movies.
